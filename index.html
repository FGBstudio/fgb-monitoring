Cookie Policy

Last updated: 01 December 2025

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit them. They allow the site to recognise your device and store information about your preferences or actions.

This Cookie Policy explains how FGB studio * Zmyrna limited uses cookies and similar technologies on www.monitoring@fgb-studio.com.com (the "Website").

2. Types of cookies we use

We may use the following categories of cookies:

2.1 Strictly necessary cookies

  • Essential for the basic functioning of the Website.
  • Enable navigation, access to secure areas and correct display of pages.
  • These cookies do not require your consent.

2.2 Preference / functionality cookies

  • Store your choices (e.g. language, region or preferences).
  • Provide a more personalised browsing experience.
  • May require your consent, depending on applicable regulations.

2.3 Analytics / performance cookies

  • Help us understand how visitors use the Website (e.g. number of visitors, most visited pages, traffic sources).
  • Used to improve the performance and content of the Website.
  • Are set only with your consent, where required by law.

2.4 Marketing / tracking cookies (if used)

  • Used to show relevant advertising on other websites or platforms, based on your browsing on our Website.
  • Typically set by third-party partners (e.g. advertising networks).
  • Used only with your explicit consent.

3. Third-party cookies

Some cookies are placed by third parties (for example, analytics or embedded content providers). These third parties may independently process data for their own purposes. We encourage you to review their privacy and cookie policies for more information.

Examples (if used on your Website):

  • Google Analytics – https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Other tools and platforms actually used by your website.

4. Cookie consent and management

When you first visit our Website, you may see a cookie banner asking you to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customise your choices

You can manage your preferences at any time by:

  • Using the cookie settings available on the Website (if implemented)
  • Adjusting your browser settings to block or delete cookies

Please note that if you disable certain cookies (especially strictly necessary cookies), some parts of the Website may not function properly.

5. How to manage cookies in your browser

You can usually configure your browser to block all cookies, accept only some types or delete cookies that have already been set. The procedures vary depending on the browser (e.g. Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). Please refer to your browser's help section for detailed instructions.

6. Retention periods

Cookies are stored on your device for different periods of time:

  • Session cookies: deleted when you close the browser.
  • Persistent cookies: remain for a specific period or until you delete them manually.

The retention time for each cookie depends on its purpose and is determined by us or the relevant third party.
